Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Goal Rush Runner ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile game.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • TikTok Account Information: When you log in with TikTok, we may receive your OpenID and basic profile information (username, avatar) if you authorize it.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Gameplay Data: Scores, achievements, player selections, and game progress
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and screen resolution
  • Usage Data: Time spent playing, features used, and session frequency

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the game experience
  • Save your game progress and preferences
  • Display leaderboards and achievements
  • Improve game performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support if needed

3. Data Storage

Your game progress and preferences are stored locally on your device and/or through TikTok's cloud storage services. We do not maintain separate servers or databases for user data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your information for marketing purposes

We may share anonymous, aggregated data (not personally identifiable) for analytics purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

The game integrates with TikTok's platform and may use:

  • TikTok's authentication services
  • TikTok's cloud storage for game saves
  • Ad services provided by TikTok (rewarded video ads)

These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

6. Children's Privacy

Goal Rush Runner is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

You can:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out of data collection by uninstalling the game

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the TikTok platform.
